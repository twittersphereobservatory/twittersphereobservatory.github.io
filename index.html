Twittersphere OBSERVATORY

The French Twittersphere Observatory (FTO) is a research-based project that uses textual analysis to track the changes in psychological well-being of a fixed representative sample of the Twittersphere of France, Italy, Spain and the UK. The FTO project involves researchers from Aix-Marseille School of Economics and from the Department of Economics of Glendon College, York University.
