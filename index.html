Twittersphere OBSERVATORY

The Twittersphere Observatory (TO) is a research-based project that uses textual analysis to track the changes in psychological well-being of a fixed representative sample of the Twittersphere of France, Italy, Spain and the UK. The TO project involves researchers from Aix-Marseille School of Economics and from the Department of Economics of Glendon College, York University. Data are updated weekly.
